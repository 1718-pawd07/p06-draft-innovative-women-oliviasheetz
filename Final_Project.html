Biography

Early Life

Valentina Tereshkova was born on March 6, 1937 in Russia to Vladimir Tereshkova, her father, and Elena Fyodorovna Tereshkova, her mother. When she was young, her dad died while fighting in World War II. She and her two siblings were raised by their mother. She worked in a textile mill and that is how she supported her children and herself. Tereshkova attended school for the first time around the age of 8 or 10.
